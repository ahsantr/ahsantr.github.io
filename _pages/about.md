I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the [Intelligent Biomimetic Design Lab](https://en.ibdl.pku.edu.cn/) at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/). Previously I was a lecturer at [Air University](https://au.edu.pk/). I completed my M.S. at [GIK Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology](https://giki.edu.pk/) in mechanical engineering in July 2021. At GIK, I was advised by [Sarvat Ahmad](https://cie.kfupm.edu.sa/people/faculty/dr-sarvat-m-ahmad/) and was a member of the [Vibrational Dynamics and Control Systems Lab](https://sites.google.com/prod/view/controllabgiki/home). I received my B.S. from [University of Engineering and Technology Peshawar](https://www.uetpeshawar.edu.pk) with highest honors in mechanical engineering in 2019. I have also worked at [King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals](https://kfupm.edu.sa) and [National Univesity of Sciences and Technology](https://nust.edu.pk).
